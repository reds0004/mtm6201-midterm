About Us

Moments & Lens is a family-oriented photography company, run by Samir Chaulagain. Samir's passion for photography began with his own family and he's excited to bring the same level of care to families in Orleans and Rockland.

Services

Moments & Lens is equipped to shoot personal and family portraits both outdoors and indoors. We are able to deliver prints of any size, up to and including 40 x 60 inches, and can rent equipment to produce larger prints upon request.
